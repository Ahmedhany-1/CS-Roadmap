<summary>General Guidelines - ÿ•ÿ±ÿ¥ÿßÿØÿßÿ™ ÿπÿßŸÖÿ©</summary>

- **Diversify Sources** ‚Äì Don't rely on just one source, listen to multiple people for broader understanding  
- **Repeat for Mastery** ‚Äì Return to videos two or three times for reinforcement  
- **Practice Before Watching Solutions** ‚Äì Try to solve problems yourself before looking at solutions  
- **Think Actively While Watching** ‚Äì Keep your mind engaged, not just passively listening

- **Apply What You Learn** ‚Äì Practice immediately with any project or simple exercises
